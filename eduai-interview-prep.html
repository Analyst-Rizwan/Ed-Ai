Negotiation Tactics
The Anchor
State your target first, higher than your real minimum. This sets the negotiating range in your favour.
The Silence
After naming your number, stop talking. Whoever speaks next loses the upper hand.
The Flinch
When given an offer, show mild disappointment (not anger). This signals room to move.
Total Package
Negotiate equity, remote days, title, and bonus — not just salary. Often easier to get.
